Lats.dev is the next companion surface for Lattices: a new iPad app and domain for controlling your Mac workspace from beside the keyboard.

The first public step is TestFlight. We want a small group of people using it with their real Mac workspaces, because the app only gets interesting when it is sitting next to the keyboard during actual work.

It is built around the gestures and controls that feel natural on a tablet: trackpad movement, window actions, shortcut decks, live workspace state, and a cockpit view that stays close while the Mac does the heavy lifting.

This is separate from the weekly Lattices release review. The release review covers what shipped across the Mac app, CLI, updater, distribution, bridge security, and architecture. Lats.dev is the thing those pieces are starting to make possible.
