lattice studio 5 explorations

Press E → the desktop dims and every window on the display lifts into a uniform lattice, clustered by app (same-app tiles adjacent, sharing a tint). Each tile wears a home-row hint letter; press it to pluck that window (toggle). Picks get a slot number — the order they'll sit in the gathered grid. ⏎ snaps the real windows into that lattice; esc bails (nothing moved). Below: the same ten windows, four visual directions. Same bones — different soul.

01 Blueprint — structural, technical, CAD-like

Hairline tinted frames on a faint engineering grid; the tint is a precise top-edge accent; hint letters are crisp mono chips. Leans hard into the “lattice” identity — feels like a layout tool, not a flashy launcher.

02 Hyperspace — glowing, energetic, “warp”

Dark vignette, tiles glow in their app tint, hint letters bloom in the center like nodes. Plucked tiles light up and a glowing numbered node marks the slot. Most “mode”-feeling — it reads as a place you warp into.

03 Frost — calm, premium, soft

Frosted translucent tiles, soft shadows, gentle pills for the hint letters, white rings for picks. The quietest of the four — calm and legible, closest to the “just chill” feel you asked for.

04 Clusters — grouping made explicit

Instead of relying on adjacency + tint, each app becomes a labeled container. Makes the “cluster by app / size / whatever” idea literal and scannable — you see the groups, not just a grid. (Swap the grouping key to size, space, or recency without touching the visuals.)

05 Converged — letters + clusters you define

The one you described: hint letters always on, clusters as the spatial frame — and clusters are yours. A cluster is just a named rule. Some are smart/auto (by app, by path), and you can define your own — here lattices is a custom cluster (path ~/dev/lattices) that pulls Code, Terminal and Figma together, mixing apps. Tint still encodes the app; the container encodes the cluster — two independent axes. Over time you grow your cluster vocabulary, and agents can draft the rules for you. Pluck a whole cluster at once, or letter-by-letter; ⏎ gathers.
